Our Story

A Legacy of
Authentic Flavors

Founded in 2009 by Chef Ravi Shankar, Spice Garden was born from a single belief: that every meal should feel like coming home. Our recipes are rooted in the royal kitchens of Hyderabad, where slow-cooked biryanis and hand-ground masalas were an art form.

Today, our kitchen carries forward that legacy — no shortcuts, no compromises. Just fire, patience, and the finest ingredients sourced directly from local farmers across Telangana.
